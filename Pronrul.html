How to pronounce Jorgån the right way.

  1. J pronounced sh

    shut it = jut it

  2. Ss pronounced ts

    Example: Dessån pronounced detsån

  3. T pronounced th

    Example: Torn pronounced thorn

  4. Sh - separate.

    Example: sasha is pronounced “sas-ha”

  5. å = long a sound

    The end of leviosa is an å

  6. jj is the same as a normal(english) j

    Josh in english is Jjoj in Jorgå

  7. Ø is the o sound in out

    Møn has the ow sound in maui(Moana)

  8. Tt is the same as english t

    tip in english is ttip in jorgå

  9. c is ch in jorgå

    cop in english is chop in jorgå
